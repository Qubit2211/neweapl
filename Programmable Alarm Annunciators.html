Programmable Alarm Annunciators

EAPL annunciators come in UL rated ABS sleek, compact, and light enclosures. The window glow slides/caps can be replaced at the site based on color requirements. For programming fault input & relay output configuration, front buttons are provided in addition to terminals at the rear.
M2 Series M3 Series Alarm Indicator

M2 Series

Features
  • Microprocessor-based design with super bright SMD LEDs
  • The sequence of operation - Manual /Auto Reset / Manual Reset with ring back/FIFO - User Selectable
  • RS-485 Mod-bus communication is available in select models
Applications

C&R panels, Transformer panels, DG set panels, Fire annunciation panels, Instrumentation panels and many more

M3 Series

Features
  • Microprocessor-based design with super bright SMD LEDs
  • Master and slave units are available in 4,6 & 8 windows
  • Incorporates a built-in buzzer in addition to fault alarm relay output.
  • User-selectable sequence of operations-Manual/Auto reset/ Manual reset with ring back / FIFO
Applications

C&R panels, Transformer panels, DG set panels, Fire annunciation panels, Instrumentation panels and many more.
