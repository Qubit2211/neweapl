Pump Controller

EAPL manufactures indigenously designed quality Pump controllers suitable for household and irrigation requirements. Enclosed in aesthetic, sturdy ABS plastic, they come in different variants to cater to 3 wired single phase pumps of different HP loads. They also feature a built-in GSM-based remote ON/OFF control system via SMS.
