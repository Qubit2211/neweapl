Digital Timer Switch

Time switches are control devices that switch ON loads with reference to real-time and then operate for a predefined duration irrespective of power failure operation.
Digital Timer Switch

A-Series

Features
  • Switches ON-OFF 4 times in a day with respect to real time
  • Manual over riding possible.
Applications

Street lighting, Advertising boards, DG sets, Pumps, Compressors, Exhaust fans, ATM air conditioners and many more
